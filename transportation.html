Transportation

There are many bus services to choose from. We have both public and private bus services. You may use our Taniti app to see the bus schedule and reserve any slots for private bus tours.
We have over 3,000 bikes around our island! You can travel to every part of the island with a bike. Explore our trails and rainforests. Use any one of the bike share apps to rent a bike with ease.
Our airport offers a car rental service, so you can travel around the island with ease. We have eco-friendly and luxury options. Reserve yours now before you travel as we run out very quickly during peek days of the year.
Our taxi services can give you a peace of mind that during your trip, we will take care of you. Our services are highly rated and our firendly drives will recommend you the best spots around the island. Use our Taniti app to reserve your time slots.
