Attractions

Things to do

Something to do for everyone

Ferries

We offer ferry rides around the island. Book a tour with our professional historians and learn about our island. Tour starts at the castle and finishes at the Merriton Landing.

Merriton Landing

If you want a one stop shop for all the attractions we offer, Merriton Landing is the place to go. You'll be able to find dance clubs, paragliding, helicopter rides, zip-lining, pubs, fishing tours, underwater activities and so much more. This is one of our fastest growing areas with many more attractions to come.

Beach Trails

If you're and adventurer, you'll love our hiking trails. The views at the top of the trail are the best views you can get on the island. It is well worth the hike for the memories you will make.
